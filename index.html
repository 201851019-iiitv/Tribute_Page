APJ Abdul Kalam

About

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, in full Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was born on 15 October 1931. His birth anniversary is observed as World Students' Day. He was the President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was honoured with several prestigious awards including India's highest civilian honour "Bharat Ratna" in 1997. He studied physics and aerospace engineering., Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India’s missile and nuclear weapons programs.He was known as the Missile Man of India . He was president of India from 2002 to 2007.
